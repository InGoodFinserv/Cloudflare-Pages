The Process

Four steps to intelligent investing

InGood's Liquidity-First engine works quietly in the background — reading, analysing, and investing — while you live your life.

01
🎚️

Set your comfort limits

Define your investment comfort zone — minimum liquidity to always hold, and the max you're okay investing. InGood respects these boundaries absolutely, every month.

02
🧠

Cash flow analysis

InGood connects to your credit card and bank account to map spending patterns, income cycles, and monthly obligations — building a precise, live financial picture of your cash flow.

03
🔍

Surplus auto-detected

Each month, the engine identifies exactly how much surplus sits beyond your spending and liquidity buffer. No guesswork, no rigid commitments — just real numbers.

04
📊

Dynamic index fund allocation

The surplus is automatically invested into low-cost index funds via your linked bank account — with the SIP amount adjusting dynamically every month based on your actual cash flow.

Why InGood

Built around your liquidity

Every feature stems from one belief: your money should stay accessible first, and grow second — automatically.

⚡

Self-Adjusting SIP — No rigid monthly commitment

Unlike traditional SIPs with fixed amounts that drain your account on tough months, InGood's SIP adapts in real-time. High-spend month? It invests less. Surplus month? It invests more. Your investment always fits your life — not the other way around.

💧

Liquidity-First Investing

InGood never compromises your liquidity. Your reserve is always protected — investments only happen from genuine surplus, never from your safety net.

Credit card + cash flow integration

Automatic surplus detection
